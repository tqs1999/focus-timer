About Pomodoro Timer Pro

Pomodoro Timer Pro is a free online productivity timer created to help students, professionals, creators, and exam aspirants improve focus and manage time effectively.

The goal of this website is to provide a simple and distraction-free study environment using the Pomodoro Technique.

Whether you are preparing for UPSC, SSC, NEET, JEE, college exams, or professional work, this timer can help improve concentration and productivity.
